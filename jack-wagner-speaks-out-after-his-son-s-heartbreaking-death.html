Jack Wagner Speaks Out After His Son's Heartbreaking Death

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 12, 2024

On July 23, 2022, Jack Wagner spoke out for the first time publicly since Harrison’s death. The actor posted a video on his Instagram thanking everyone for the support he and his family have received. “Thank you all so much for the love and kindness that you’ve sent. I can’t tell you how much it’s meant to me,” adding “It’s helped me more than you’ll ever know.”

In the video, Jack is at an airport, revealing he was on his way to Vancouver to start filming Season 10 of the Hallmark Channel show “When Calls the Heart” that he has starred in since 2014 (via IMDb). On returning to work, the actor said, “I’m beyond excited and grateful for that. Hopefully it will make you laugh and we can enjoy it together,” and added that he will share behind-the-scenes footage during shooting.

Kristina Wagner has yet to return to “General Hospital” where she reprised her role as Felicia Jones earlier this year (via Soaps.com), but the cast and crew of the show have overwhelmed the Wagner family with support. Laura Wright, who plays Carly Corinthos on the soap, said the “General Hospital” family “will be there with open arms” as soon as the actress is ready to come back (via ET Online). Kristina has yet to speak publicly since Harrison’s death, but fans have been sending their love and condolences to the family as they grieve in private.

Little Mix Receive Critical Acclaim On Their Whitney Houston &amp; Jason Derulo Cover

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 20, 2024

Little Mix got soul!

Earlier today, the talented Little Mix were put on the spotlight when BBC Radio 1 posted their BBC Radio 1 Live Lounge session cover of a gospel-infused mash-up between Jason Derulo’s “Want To Want Me” and Whitney Houston’s “I Wanna Dance With Somebody”.

Not only did the performance showcase their immense talent as a group but their bond as a girl group. These girls have natural talent and it obviously shows in stripped-back performances like these.

The performance received praise and acclaim by numerous sites and fans alike including Kevin McHale who stated this on his Twitter: “Watching this again & again & again. @LittleMix just got it!”.

You don’t want to miss this Live Lounge session so check it out below!

Who knows, after watching you might just become a little mixer yourself. Enjoy!

Eat Like a Star: Celeb Diet Plans

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Sunfare

Desperate Housewives‘ Eva Longoria takes these pre-packaged meals and snacks with her on set. Starting at $25 per day, the program’s packages contain two snacks and three meals (totaling 1,200 calories a day), and can be easily delivered to your front door. “It counts [calories] for you!” Longoria says.

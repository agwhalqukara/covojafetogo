What Is Scott Disicks Net Worth? Facts On His Age, Family, Education

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 5, 2024

The dating affairs of Scott Disick often occupies a significant place in the news media. The father of three is popular among fans from his on-and-off relationship with Kourtney Kardashian.

Scott Disick, age 37, is an American television personality known for his appearances on the reality show, Keeping Up with the Kardashians. He was in a relationship with Kourtney Kardashian for nine years and now shares three children together.

Scott Disick Bio; Parents, Family, Education

Scott Michael Disick was born on 26 May 1983 in Eastport, Long Island, New York. He is the only son of his parents, Jeffrey Disick and Bonnie Disick. His father died at age 63 on 28 October 2013 and just after two months, his father died at age 63 on 3d January 2014.

Disick’s parents raised him mostly from the wealth inherited from his grandfather. His paternal grandfather David M. Disick is a well-known attorney and a real-estate businessman. He is the founding principal of The Fractional Consultant, based in Orlando, Florida.

Scott attended the Ross School in East Hampton, Long Island. However, it is known that he has not completed his high school education.

What Is Scott Disick’s Ethnicity?

Scott Disick is of Ashkenazi Jewish and German ethnicity from his family side. Both of his paternal grandparents were born to Russian Jewish parents.

What Is His Job?

Scott has been appearing on numerous reality shows including Keeping Up With the Kardashians, Kourtney and Khloé Take Miami, and Rob and Chyna. In August 2019, he began appearing on his new reality show Flip It Like Disick, which airs on E! network.

Earlier, Scott was a male model, who appeared on the Heartland young adult novel series. Later, he launched a product for men, Monte Carlo, under the tagline ‘It’s like liquid sunshine’.

Disick has invested in numerous business ventures and launched various products such as Rejuvacare and QuickTrim. Meanwhile, in 2012, he opened a Japanese meatpacking district restaurant, Ryu, in New York City in partnership. Nevertheless, the business closed after 191 days of operation.

In the summer of 2018, he launched his own clothing line, Talentless. The company produces comfort wears such as T-shirts, hoodies, and cargo pants.

How Much Is His Net Worth?

Well, Scott comes from a wealthy family. His grandfather is a well-known businessman and his father was a real estate developer. He generates revenues in millions from his clothing line and others.

Disick now enjoys the lavish lifestyle, often evinced on his show Flip It Like Disick and his Instagram posts. From the multiple business ventures, he now has the net worth of an estimated $40 million.

How Scott Met His Girlfriend Kourtney?

The 37-year-old was in a long term relationship with his reality TV star girlfriend, Kourtney Kardashian. The duo first met at their mutual friend Joe Francis’ house party in Mexico. Eventually, they began dating in 2006 and their on-and-off love affair became the major storyline in the Keeping Up With The Kardashians series.

Well, Scott and Kourtney are raising three kids together; Mason Dash Disick (born 14 December 2009), Penelope Scotland Disick (born 8 July 2012), and Reign Aston Disick (born 14 December 2014). The pair, who have numerous relationship issues, finally split in 2015. However, they are in good terms and often appear together.

In 2017, Scott began dating Sofia Richie, the young daughter of Lionel Richie. The couple dated for three years and recently ended their relationship in May 2020.

Some Interesting Facts About Scott Disick

  • Scott was arrested by police at the age of 18 for crashing his car in Riverhead, New York after being drunk in 2001.
  • He is often known by the nicknames; Lord Disick, Scotty LD, Lord of the Night, and Lord of the Manor.
  • His grandfather is a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania Law School. He published a book named Fractional Vacation Homes: Marketing and Sales in Challenging Times in 2010.
  • His car collection includes Bentley Mulsanne, Ferrari 812 Superfast, Lamborghini Aventador, and Rolls-Royce Phantom.

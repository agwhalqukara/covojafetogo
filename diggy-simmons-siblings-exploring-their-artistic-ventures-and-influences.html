Diggy Simmons Siblings: Exploring Their Artistic Ventures and Influences

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 19, 2024

Diggy Simmons siblings unveiled. Find out who they are.

These days, Daniel “Diggy” Simmons III is primarily recognized for his acting role on the popular sitcom Grown-ish, rather than his music career.

However, his musical journey commenced with his debut mixtape titled “The First Flight,” which gained significant attention, accumulating over 100,000 downloads from his personal blog.

What made this accomplishment noteworthy was that it was uncommon for an emerging artist to achieve such impressive numbers independently, without the support of a record label.

Consequently, Atlantic Records took notice and eagerly pursued this young talent.

Diggy holds the distinction of being the son of Joseph Simmons, also known as Rev Run, one of the co-founders of the influential hip-hop group Run-DMC.

The Simmons family gained further recognition through the MTV reality show titled Run’s House, which provided a glimpse into their lives.

The show proved to be a tremendous success, airing for 59 episodes and spanning six seasons.

Diggy Simmons 5 siblings from the oldest to the youngest:

1. Vanessa Simmons

Vanessa Simmons, born on August 5, 1983, is an accomplished actress and TV host hailing from the United States.

As of 2023, she is 39 years old.

Vanessa has made a name for herself in the entertainment industry, particularly through her involvement in the popular show Project Runway: Threads.

Her talent and presence have captivated audiences, earning her recognition and acclaim.

With her diverse skills and charismatic personality, Vanessa Simmons continues to make significant contributions to the world of television and entertainment.

Vanessa Simmons, the eldest daughter of Joseph Simmons and Valerie Vaughn, is recognized by Run-DMC fans as a familiar face from their MTV reality show in the early 2000s.

In addition to her television roles, Vanessa is a versatile talent, working as an actress and producer.

She has showcased her acting skills in notable films like Dysfunctional Friends, Boogie Town, Happiness, and in the TV series Monogamy, where she portrayed the character Caroline.

Vanessa’s creativity and passion extend beyond the screen, as she collaborates with her sister Angela in designing shoes for their brand Pastry.

In a surprising turn of events, Vanessa demonstrated her adventurous spirit by participating in the Miss California USA pageant in 2008 under the alias “Vanessa Jean.”

While she didn’t win the crown, her initiative and bravery would undoubtedly make her a favorite for a trophy celebrating those qualities.

Vanessa Simmons consistently keeps herself engaged in various endeavors, mirroring the dynamic nature of her father, Joseph Simmons.

2. Angela Simmons

Angela Simmons, born on September 18, 1987, is a renowned shoe designer hailing from Queens, New York.

As of 2023, she is 35 years old.

Angela has made a significant impact in the fashion industry with her exceptional talent and creativity as a shoe designer.

She is widely recognized for her work as a designer for Pastry Shoes, a brand that she co-founded with her sister.

Angela’s designs have garnered attention and admiration from fashion enthusiasts worldwide.

With her dedication to the craft and remarkable skills, Angela Simmons continues to leave her mark as a prominent figure in the world of shoe design.

Angela Simmons is a notable figure in the online sphere, renowned as both an internet personality and a talented shoe designer.

She dedicates her time to designing for Pastry, a brand she is involved with, while also managing her own YouTube channel called Just Angela.

With a substantial following of nearly 40,000 subscribers, Angela’s channel covers a diverse range of subjects.

Her videos touch on various topics, including insightful content on motherhood, mental health, business, fitness, food, beauty, relationships, and regular updates on her life as both a mother and a member of the esteemed Simmons family.

Tragically, Angela Simmons experienced the heartbreaking loss of her fiancé, Sutton Tennyson, who was the father of their 5-year-old son.

In 2018, Sutton was shot and killed, leaving Angela to navigate the challenges of single motherhood.

The assailant responsible for this tragic incident received a life sentence for the crime.

Despite enduring such a profound loss, Angela Simmons continues to share her journey, offering inspiration and resilience to her audience while honoring the memory of her late fiancé.

3. Joseph Simmons Jr.

Joseph Simmons Jr., born on September 29, 1989, gains recognition as an actor and rapper, showcasing his talents in multiple domains.

As of 2023, he is 33 years old.

Joseph, hailing from New York, New York, has made a mark in the entertainment industry with his exceptional skills and creativity.

Additionally, Joseph has ventured into the world of music, demonstrating his skills as a rapper.

Joseph Simmons Jr. continues to make waves in the entertainment world, with his dedication and passion driving his success, particularly in his notable role as “King of Newark”.

Joseph Simmons Jr., also known by his rap alias Jojo Simmons, is the oldest son of Joseph Simmons and Valerie Vaughn.

At 33 years old, Jojo Simmons is a respected producer, reality TV personality, and influential figure in the entertainment industry.

He gained recognition through appearances on Run’s House and Growing Up Hip Hop.

Jojo also displayed his acting prowess in notable films like King of Newark, King of Newark 2, and Fade Away.

He is happily married to Tanice Amira, and the couple has been blessed with two children.

Jojo actively engages with his audience through his Instagram account, where he has amassed a substantial following of 543,000 followers.

On his social media platform, he provides glimpses into his life as a dedicated family man and entrepreneur.

4. Diggy Simmons

Diggy Simmons, born on March 21, 1995, is a multi-talented individual recognized for his work as an actor, rapper, and songwriter.

As of 2023, he is 28 years old.

Hailing from Queens, New York, Diggy has made significant contributions to the entertainment industry.

He gained prominence for his role in the popular television show Grown-ish, where his acting skills shone brightly.

Additionally, Diggy has showcased his lyrical prowess as a rapper and songwriter, captivating audiences with his unique style and creativity.

With his versatile talents and undeniable passion, Diggy Simmons continues to leave a lasting impact in the world of entertainment.

Diggy Simmons, widely recognized as the most popular member among the Simmons siblings, has achieved remarkable success as a singer, songwriter, and rapper.

He has collaborated with renowned artists such as Lupe Fiasco and became a member of the esteemed rap group, All City Chess Club.

Diggy showcased talent in both music and acting, notably through his role in Grown-ish.

Early on, industry experts recognized him as a promising rap star, and he earned the distinction of being named one of Billboard’s “Artists to Watch 2011.”

He embarked on tours such as the BET 106 & Park Closer to My Dreams Tour, sharing the stage with rising stars like Mac Miller, Lil Twist, and Tyga.

Diggy prefers to forge his own musical path, disregarding advice from his family, including his prominent rap figure father.

5. Russell Simmons II

Russell Simmons II, born on August 30, 1997, excels in rap, singing, songwriting, and acting.

At the age of 25, Russell has already made a name for himself.

Hailing from Bergen County, New Jersey, he gained recognition through appearances on “Rev Runs Around the World.”

Russell impresses in entertainment with diverse skills and a passion for the arts, captivating audiences.


Russell Simmons II, also known as Russy, actively engages in acting, rapping, and music, showcasing his diverse range of talents.

He gained visibility on his father’s shows, Rev Runs Around the World and Rev Run’s Sunday Suppers.

Understandably, considering his father’s legacy as a Run-DMC founding member, he hasn’t achieved their musical recognition.

Run-DMC’s music from 1983 to 2002 has influenced generations, boasting award-winning albums and chart-topping singles.

6. Victoria Anne Simmons

Victoria Anne Simmons, born on September 26, 2006, is a young individual hailing from the United States.

The youngest among the Simmons siblings was Victoria Anne Simmons.

Unfortunately, Victoria’s life was tragically cut short on the same day she was born due to a condition called omphalocele.

This genetic defect leads to the growth of internal organs outside the body.

In their book “Old School Love: And Why It Works,” Joseph and Justine Simmons shared their heartbreaking loss.

As part of their healing journey, they chose to adopt their daughter Miley Justine.

Each day, she worked towards healing her physical and emotional pain, longing for the child she had lost.

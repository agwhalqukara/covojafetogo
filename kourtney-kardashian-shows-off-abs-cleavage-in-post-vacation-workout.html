Kourtney Kardashian Shows Off Abs, Cleavage in Post-Vacation Workout

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Can’t stop, won’t stop. Kourtney Kardashian transitioned straight from the beach life in St. Barts and into beast mode back in Los Angeles on Saturday, Aug. 22, sharing a selfie of herself looking in tip-top shape on Instagram.

“No breaks,” she captioned the shot, in which she wears a cleavage-baring black sports bra, figure-hugging black tights, and matching sneakers. The mom-of-three shows off her impressive abs as she poses in the mirror.

Just two days prior, Kardashian, 36, showed off her enviable bikini body in a yellow and gray two-piece by Agent Provocateur while walking the beach with half-sister Kendall Jenner.

The reality star, who split from longtime boyfriend Scott Disick last month, has showed no signs of slowing down since their headline-making breakup. In addition to her recent getaway to St. Barts with the Kardashian-Jenner clan, the eldest Kardashian sister has also been busy taking care of son Mason, 5, daughter Penelope, and little Reign, 8 months.

Meanwhile, Disick, 32, has been spending his time post-split partying in Mexico with his guy pals.

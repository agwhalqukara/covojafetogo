Critics' Choice Television Awards 2015: Complete Winners List

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

It's a big night for the small screen! TV's best and brightest headed to the Beverly Hilton Hotel on Sunday, May 31, for the 2015 Critics' Choice Television Awards hosted by So You Think You Can Dance star Cat Deeley. (Tune in to A&E at 8 p.m. ET/5 p.m. PT to watch!)

Presented annually by the Broadcast Television Journalists Association since 2011, the event honors critically acclaimed shows and performances from the past year, with nominees in categories like "Best Comedy Series," "Best Drama Series," and "Best Reality Series."

FX's Justified and HBO's Olive Kitteridge lead the pack with five nominations each, followed closely by The Americans, Bessie, The Good Wife, Transparent, and Wolf Hall, each with four nominations. Us Weekly will be updating the winners list below, so check back throughout the night for more!

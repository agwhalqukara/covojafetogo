Top 10 Greta Van Fleet most popular songs

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Which are the Greta Van Fleet most popular songs?

Greta Van Fleet has risen rapidly to become one of the world’s most beloved rock and roll bands.

They have unveiled two EPs, “Black Smoke Rising” and “From The Fires,” as well as one LP, “Anthem Of The Peaceful Army.”

Below are Greta Van Fleet’s top ten songs to date.

1. “Highway Tune”

The “Highway Tune” was the song that ignited the hype surrounding Greta Van Fleet.

The single was unveiled in the form of a music video, which garnered immense success.

Regardless of whether you appreciate its Led Zeppelin-esque sound or not, “Highway Tune” sparked conversations about Greta Van Fleet and played a significant role in propelling the band to fame.

2. “Black Smoke Rising”

“Black Smoke Rising” is the eponymous title track of Greta Van Fleet’s debut EP.

It’s astonishing how much hype the band was able to create with just four tracks, and “Black Smoke Rising” has become a fan-favorite sing-along at live shows.

3. “Safari Song”

“Safari Song” was showcased on the “Black Smoke Rising” EP and served as the lead track on “From The Fires.”

This lively rock number features lead singer Josh Kiszka channeling his inner Robert Plant, delivering an energetic performance that has become a signature of the band’s style.

4. “When The Curtain Falls”

“When The Curtain Falls” was Greta Van Fleet’s first single from “Anthem of the Peaceful Army” and proved to be a massive hit for the band.

5. “You’re The One”

“You’re The One” is a track from “Anthem of the Peaceful Army” and was also released as a single.

It’s not a hard-rocking number, but rather a mellower, acoustic track that features a catchy sing-along chorus.

6. “Anthem”

“Anthem” is the titular track from Greta Van Fleet’s “Anthem of the Peaceful Army,” and its message revolves around putting aside differences and uniting through the power of music.

7. “Age of Man”

“Age Of Man” could be considered the most “epic” track from “Anthem of the Peaceful Army.”

With a running time of just over six minutes, the song feels like a time machine that transports listeners back to the 1970s with its vintage sound and grandiose scale.

8. “Lover, Leaver”

“Greta Van Fleet” had been performing “Lover, Leaver” (Taker, Believer) live for some time before it was eventually included on “Anthem of the Peaceful Army.”

It’s undoubtedly a highlight of the album, showcasing the band’s signature sound and high-energy performance style.

9. “Edge of Darkness”

“Edge of Darkness” is a track featured on “From The Fires” and appropriately titled, as the song has a slightly darker vibe compared to other tracks by Greta Van Fleet.

With its strong ’70s rocker sound, it’s a standout track on the EP.

10. “Watching Over”

“Watching Over” is a mesmerizing psychedelic jam track that showcases impressive guitar work.

This song was among the initial tracks released from “Anthem of the Peaceful Army.”

Thats the end of our  10 most popular Greta Van Fleet most popular songs. Enjoy

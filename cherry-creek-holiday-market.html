Cherry Creek Holiday Market

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024
Cherry Creek North - Fillmore Plaza105 Fillmore StreetDenver CO 80206
Google Map

Notes regarding time

Hours vary by day

Description

The elves have nothing on Cherry Creek North. Holiday shopping should always be this fun! Join us for the 4th annual Holiday Market and spend the day wandering under the lights on Fillmore Plaza, listening to live music and getting into the seasonal spirit as you discover one-of-a-kind pieces from over 80 local makers, designers, and creators. 

Market Hours

Sunday-Wednesday - 12:00pm - 7:00pm

Thursday-Saturday - 11:00am - 9:00pm

Thanksgiving, November 23, 12:00pm-3:00pm

Christmas Eve, December 24, 12:00pm-3:00pm

Closed 11/20, 11/27, 12/4

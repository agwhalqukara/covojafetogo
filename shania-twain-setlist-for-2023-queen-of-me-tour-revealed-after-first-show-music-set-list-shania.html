Shania Twain Setlist for 2023 Queen of Me Tour Revealed After First Show! | Music, Set LIst, Shania

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Shania Twain has officially kicked off her Queen of Me Tour and you can check out the full set list right here!

The 57-year-old singer is celebrating her 2023 studio album Queen of Me with a global tour, which began on Friday night (April 28) at Spokane Arena in Spokane, Wash.

Songs from the night included “You’re Still the One,” “Waking Up Dreaming,” “That Don’t Impress Me Much,” and “Whose Bed Have Your Boots Been Under.”

Shania will be on the road for the rest of the year, hitting stops around the United States, Canada, Ireland and the United Kingdom.

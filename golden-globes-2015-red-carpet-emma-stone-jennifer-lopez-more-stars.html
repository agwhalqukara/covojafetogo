Golden Globes 2015 Red Carpet: Emma Stone, Jennifer Lopez, More Stars

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Time to announce the winners—of the red carpet, that is!

Jennifer Lopez, Jared Leto, Emily Blunt, and more stepped out in style on Sunday, Jan. 11, to the 2015 Golden Globes in Beverly Hills—and, oh, did they look glam!

See Us Weekly‘s favorite ensembles of the night, from the fiercest lady in red to the best-dressed couple. Read on—and don’t forget to tell Us your favorite!

Best Surprise Details: Emma Stone 

The Best Supporting Actress nominee’s bedazzled Lanvin jumpsuit had all the right details: a rhinestone-embellished bodice, pockets in the pants, and a big ol’ bow cinching her waist. Plus, the Birdman actress added even more shine via a glittering barrette in her tousled ‘do. 

Best Statement Earrings: Emily Blunt

Jennifer Aniston and Salma Hayek brought their earring A-game, too, in bold danglers. But the Into the Woods actress’ intricate, turquoise Lorraine Schwartz stunners especially popped alongside her blue peepers.

Best Cleavage: Jessica Chastain

We love you, J.Lo and Kate Hudson, but this one goes to Chastain! The A Most Violent Year actress arrived on the red carpet flaunting her perky assets in a liquid bronze Versace number that was tailored to a T.

Best Cape: Jennifer Lopez

But that doesn’t mean Lopez was a total shutout! Following in the footsteps of Lupita Nyong’o and her scarlet cape in 2014, the international pop star sported a bold, caped creation by Zuhair Murad (not to mention its plunging neckline and up-to-there slit). Honorable mention: Girls actress Jemima Kirke.

Best Braid: Jared Leto

Apologies to Blunt and Downton Abbey‘s Joanne Froggatt (whose emotional acceptance speech is still tearing us up!). But when Leto’s glorious ombre mane is involved, it always wins—especially when it’s styled into the perfect plait.

Best Red Carpet Camouflage: Viola Davis

The How to Get Away With Murder lead was the fiercest red carpet match, thanks to her stunning Donna Karan dress, dipped in ruby and sprinkled with rhinestones along the neckline. And it was clearly the hue of the night! More lovely ladies in red: Allison Williams, Taylor Schilling, Julianne Margulies, Heidi Klum, and Jane Fonda.

Best-Dressed Couple: Eddie Redmayne and Hannah Bagshawe

There was lots of love on the red carpet at the 2015 Globes! But one newlywed couple radiated glam amore stronger than all the rest: Globe-winning Redmayne, dapper once more in his beloved velvet, and his wife (of just a few weeks!), who looked glam in a chic-yet-understated olive dress.

Best Shine: Dakota Johnson

The Globes is a great place to sparkle—it’s got a metal right in its title! Not a fact lost on the Fifty Shades of Grey star, who dazzled in a Chanel dress, sequined from top to bottom. We think Christian Grey would approve!

Hayden Panettiere Breast Implants Plastic Surgery Before and After

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 23, 2024

[ads1 id=”alignright”]Last year, when Hayden Panettiere had made an appearance at one particular red carpet event, the beautiful petite actress was wearing a gorgeous black dress.

The dress she was wearing had a rather deep neckline, covered only with a see-through black material, which made a lot of her cleavage visible.

There was something slightly ‘off’ about her breasts which now looked to be slightly bigger than before.

Hayden had long been suspected of having went under the knife for breast augmentation, and it all seemed clear that day.

Rumors had been circulating that Hayden may have gotten breast implants in 2012, and apparently, the alleged surgery had been botched, or at least that’s what people say. If the alleged breast augmentation surgery had not been botched, then her breasts would not have appeared so separated from each other, creating somewhat of a cavity on her chest.

To make matters worse for herself, Hayden did not stop from flaunting her cleavage. So did she go under the knife to get herself an improved pair of breasts or not?

By comparing recent pictures of Hayden with pictures of her younger self, we are bound to see a slight difference in both the shape and the size of her breasts.

Hayden Panettiere Breast Implants Plastic Surgery Before and After

Dr. Leslie H. Stevens, a Beverly Hills-based plastic surgeon, believes that Hayden’s plastic surgeon may have placed her saline breast implants too close to the “middle of her sternum.” As a result of that, her breasts had been appearing puckered and rippled.

He believes that the doctor who performed the surgery may have placed the implants directly under the skin by cutting her pectoral muscle. As a result, her implants did not have enough soft tissue to be covered with and thus, the aftereffects were in plain sight.

Apparently, rippled breast implants may also occur when extremely large implants are used but sufficient breast tissue is not available and follow-up surgery can help correct it. This might just be what happened to Hayden if we are to believe the rumors that have been circulating around her in these past few years.

The fact that her breasts no longer appear to be as large as before, and the ripples are no longer there as of lately, so it could mean that Hayden Panettiere may have undergone revision breast augmentation. Congratulations to her since her breasts fit her athletic frame and look better.

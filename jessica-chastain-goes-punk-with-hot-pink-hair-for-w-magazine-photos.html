Jessica Chastain Goes Punk With Hot-Pink Hair for W Magazine: Photos

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 19, 2024

Who’s that girl?! Jessica Chastain covers the latest issue of W magazine, and while the Oscar-nominated actress has transformed plenty of times on the big screen, you’ve never seen her like this before.

In half of the shots, the natural redhead, 38, rocks long, hot-pink hair, bold blush, a blood-hued statement lip, and drawn-on brows—all a far cry from her pristine red carpet beauty choices! The other half of the photos feature Chastain modeling a platinum blonde pompadour hairstyle.

As for the clothing, you can kiss her preferred ball gowns goodbye! The Martian actress works a leather jacket by Givenchy, a lace (nipple-baring!) dress (also by the French fashion house), and a fuchsia take on the feather-topped Proenza Schouler dress a pregnant Kim Kardashian wore to the 2015 CFDA Fashion Awards this past June.

Alongside the spread, which is appropriately titled “Transformer,” Chastain, 38, talked about her upcoming role in Crimson Peak, as well as her dream job. “One of my goals is to play a villain in a Bond film. People ask me if I want to be a Bond girl, and I say, ‘No, I want to be the villain.’ I’m waiting for that call!”

The actress, who’s notoriously tight-lipped about her private life, also offered a touch about her boyfriend, Gian Luca Passi de Preposulo, whom she’s been dating for the past four years. “He’s a gentleman, and that’s very important to me,” Chastain told the mag. “He’s from an old-school Italian family. No one in his family has ever been divorced!”

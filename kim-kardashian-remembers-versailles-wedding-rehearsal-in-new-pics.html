Kim Kardashian Remembers Versailles Wedding Rehearsal in New Pics

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 2, 2024

Fairytale pic.twitter.com/ZOm5tW3ce5

— Kim Kardashian West (@KimKardashian) May 24, 2015

It's already been a year! Sunday, May 24 marks the one-year wedding anniversary of Kim Kardashian and Kanye West, but the night before, May 23, will also hold a special place in their hearts (and on her Twitter feed). 

Kisses pic.twitter.com/n0jxnps77g

— Kim Kardashian West (@KimKardashian) May 24, 2015

To commemorate the anniversary of their opulent Versailles Palace rehearsal dinner, the mom of North West posted a series of never-before-seen photos from the magical evening with her then-fiance. 

Carriage ride at Versailles pic.twitter.com/f6xl4WK4Rh

— Kim Kardashian West (@KimKardashian) May 24, 2015

From smooching in the ornate carriage to West giving one of his signature verbose toasts, Kardashian, 34, recreated the evening with a series of shots for her almost 32 million followers. 

Bae in Paris ???? pic.twitter.com/TDHCq63i9K

— Kim Kardashian West (@KimKardashian) May 24, 2015

For the special event, the couple wore matching Margiela looks with the reality star in a low-cut white dress and her rapper love in a camel suit.

I'm feelin' our Margiela looks #WeddingWeek #Versailles pic.twitter.com/VKMhu77qJZ

— Kim Kardashian West (@KimKardashian) May 24, 2015

Kimye's actual wedding took place at the 16th-century Forte di Belvedere in Italy, and Us Weekly exclusively revealed that that pair plans to renew their vows this year at the Eiffel Tower. West rented out the historic Parisian landmark and the 58 Tour Eiffel restaurant for the occasion.  

Welcoming speech! #Versailles #OneYearAgo #WeddingWeek pic.twitter.com/lRaHswOt5M

— Kim Kardashian West (@KimKardashian) May 24, 2015

Earlier in the day, Kardashian shared photos from the family's brunch at Valentino's mansion and her Parisian bachelorette party. 

Memories from Versailles pic.twitter.com/xoUxtP4KWu

"We are forever grateful for these memories! This was the best week of my life!" she tweeted.

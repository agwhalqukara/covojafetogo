Mindy Kaling Puts On A Stylish Display In Twin Fantasy Snakeskin Outfit

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Mindy Kaling continues to show off her gorgeous style on her Instagram account where the multi-hyphenate has more than 5.7 million followers. She looked amazing wearing an outfit by Twin Fantasy in a blue, gold, and silver snakeskin print outfit and fans are curious about the look. The 41-year-old mother of two posted the pictures and has earned a reputation of being a fashionista. Mindy revealed that she gave birth to her second child during the Coronavirus pandemic and fans are amazed at how quickly she bounced back into shape.

Mindy wore the Twin Fantasy Cropped Tank and matching Track Pants. The Twin Fantasy Cropped Tank normally retails for approximately $195. The top is sleeveless and cropped. It has a round, crew neck and is made from a blend of polyamide and elastane. the print is called the multi snake.

The track pants retail for approximately $390 and is currently on sale for about $156 at Shop Bop.

Mindy Kaling currently has six projects in development and finished filming the movie Lockdown. She is currently working on the following projects: Legally Blonde 3, Who Thought That Was a Good Idea?, Venice Beach Venture, an Untitled Lang Fisher /NBC Project (TV movie), Untitled Indian Wedding Project, and Good In Bed. As someone who is always working, even throughout the pandemic, Mindy needs to ensure that she is dressed comfortably and ready for any task that is at hand.

Mindy posed for several photos where she wore the Twins Fantasy outfit. She stood in her living room and posed for the snap while giving the camera a sultry stare. She wore a pair of Jimmy Choo shoes and large, hoop earrings. Mindy wore jewelry from Brinker & Eliza as well as Suzanne Kalan. You may see a photo of Mindy wearing the Twins Fantasy outfit below.

Mindy shared a second photo where she turned to the side and showed off the look.

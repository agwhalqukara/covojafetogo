Jessica Simpson Looks Skinny on Date Night, Talks about Kids

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 5, 2024

Jessica Simpson and Eric Johnson were keeping the romance alive in their marriage on Tuesday, Feb. 17 by stepping out without the kids for a date night a deux.

But, despite Simpson's sexy outfit, which showcased her slim shoulders and skinny arms, the proud parents fell into the predictable parenting trap of talking about (and looking through pictures of) their two adorable kids!

The saucy pair, who shared some raunchy black and white snaps via Instagram the day after Valentine's Day (on Feb. 15), gave fans a peek into their private date night with a photo of Simpson, smoldering for her husband as they waited for their meal to arrive in a restaurant.

"Come On Baby, Light My Fire," she captioned the snap, which showed her playing with her cell phone and also displayed Johnson's impressive table-top art work.

But just an hour later, talk had returned to their adorable children Maxwell, 2, and Ace, 19 months, as Simpson explained what she and her husband were up to.

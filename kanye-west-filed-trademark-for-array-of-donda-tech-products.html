Kanye West Filed Trademark For Array Of Donda Tech Products

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 15, 2024

It’s been just reported that Kanye West filed a trademark for an array of Donda products. Check out the available message below.

The Shade Room said that ‘proving that he’s constantly working to add to his billionaire status, #KanyeWest now apparently has plans to take over the world of technology and he already has a list of ways to do it. Recent documents reveal that Kanye West has filed trademarks for a collection of “Donda” technology products that include everything from smartphones to tablets.’

TSR said that ‘@RollingStone reports, although he released his record-breaking album “Donda” that topped the charts a little over a month ago and just dropped his Yeezy x Gap hoodie collection, Kanye West is not slowing down at all. In his latest vision for his multi-billion empire, Ye has set his sights on conquering the world of tech—and he’s already filed trademarks to do so.’

TSR continued and said: ‘His Mascotte Holdings company (and subsidiary of Donda) filed a trademark application late last month via the United States Patent and Trademark Office. The products included in the trademark application include tablets, surround sound systems, headphones, virtual reality headsets, smartphones, USB flash drives and protective phone cases and other electronic equipment.’

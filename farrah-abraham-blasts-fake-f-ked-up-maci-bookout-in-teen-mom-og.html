Farrah Abraham Blasts &quot;Fake, F--ked Up&quot; Maci Bookout in Teen Mom OG

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 26, 2024

The gloves are off! It’s been several weeks since Farrah Abraham returned to Teen Mom OG, but the dust is still settling. Farrah’s costar Maci Bookout had a strong, negative reaction initially, first threatening to quit the show before deciding to return, but still keeping her son Bentley off the air. 

In a new sneak peek from the MTV reality series, Farrah reacts to Maci’s response, ranting to the show’s producer about the Tennessee mother. 

“It’s been six years. I’m in a way different place, I’m in a way better place,” the former porn star exclaims. “She’s in the same place she was in six years ago. Why would I be offended? I think it’s just as easy to not work with a fake, f—ked up, piece of s—t person. I’ve dealt with too much stupid s—t for the last couple of years and I don’t have time for it. I don’t have time to defend myself because I know what I hear is ignorance.”

The reality star, who appeared in an adult film called Farrah Superstar: Backdoor Teen Mom, is tired of defending her choices.
